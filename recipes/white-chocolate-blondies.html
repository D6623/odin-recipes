White Chocolate Blondies

Description

These white chocolate brownies have become an office favourite.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces of white chocolate, chopped
  • 1/2 cup of butter, softened
  • 1 1/4 cups of all purpose flour
  • 3/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/3 cup of white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 cup of semisweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit (175 degrees celsius). Grease a 9x9-inch baking pan.
  2. Melt together white chocolate and butter in the top of a double boiler over bearly simmering water; stir occasionally until smooth. Set aside to cool.
  3. Combine flour and salt in a medium bowl; set aside.
  4. Beat eggs in a large bowl with an electric mixer until foamy. Gradually add sugar and vanilla with the mixture still running. Drizzle in white chocolate mixture; mix well. Stir in flour mixture with a rubber spatula or wooden spoon. Fold in chocolate chips. Spread batter evenly into the prepared pan.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the middle comes out clean, about 25 minutes. Cool in the pan on a wire rack before cutting into bars.
  6. Enjoy!
